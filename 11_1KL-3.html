One day, our Bibles might be taken away, or we might not have access to them. The precious words found inside its pages can never be taken from us, though. The words you have hidden in your hearts will be there forever and will carry you through any challenges and joys ahead.

Challenge:

“Hide” as many words from the Bible in your heart as you can, every day.

The Bible will always be your true light in this dark world. Never forget that. It can answer any question, calm any fear, satisfy your deepest need, and lead you to a loving God. It will inform you and inflame you. Like the Reformers, you can also be an agent of light to this world.
