EXTENSION: Inductive Bible Study 1: Claims of the Bible

3 For use in Animated/Engaged Learning.

Source: David Asscherick, Bible Boot Camp, 2010

  1. What is the purpose of Scripture? (2 Tim. 3:15)
  2. How much Scripture is “breathed,’’ or inspired, by God? (2 Tim. 3:16)
  3. Is Scripture up to the task of bringing believers to spiritual maturity? (2 Tim. 3:17)
  4. What significant word is found in 2 Peter 1:19?
  5. What is the first thing to know about Scripture? (2 Pet. 1:20)
  6. Why is this the case? (2 Pet. 1:21)
  7. What claim does God make about Himself in Isaiah 46:9?
  8. What evidence is given for this claim? (Isa. 46:10)
  9. Why does Jesus tell certain things in advance of their occurrence? (John 14:29)
  10. What are some biblical prophecies you know of?
