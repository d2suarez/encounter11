Psalm 119:104, 105

“Through Your precepts I get understanding;

Therefore I hate every false way.

Your word is a lamp to my feet

And a light to my path.”

Discuss

  • What does this passage really mean?
  • How can the Bible keep us on the right path?
  • If light is a metaphor for the Bible, which of these three images do you think best depicts how our society views the Bible today, and why?
