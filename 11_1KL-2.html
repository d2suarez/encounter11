Discuss

  • What is the message of this drama?

  • How does it relate to us and the Bible?

Like the drama, we are all in a war zone. We receive “messages” from time to time from different sources. The messages in God’s Word are the one thing that will help us to keep our eyes on Jesus in this battlefield. They tell us that being separated from Him doesn’t change His love for us. We can choose to leave our Bibles unopened or hold on to these messages until we can be reunited again.
