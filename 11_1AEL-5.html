Inspiration

Inspiration means literally to “breathe in.” This infers that God has “breathed” into human writers to share His thoughts with us.

  • From what you already know, what does the Bible claim about itself?

Look up the following verses in your Bible:

2 Timothy 3:16 2 Peter 1:20, 21
Hebrews 1:1; 4:12 Romans 3:2
Proverbs 30:5, 6 Exodus 17:14
Joshua 1:8 Jeremiah 31:33
Psalm 119:11, 105 1 Thessalonians 2:13
John 5:39 John 17:17
  1. Select three of these verses to copy into your Encounter Journals.
  2. After you have copied three verses, write a short comment about what the Bible claims about itself.
