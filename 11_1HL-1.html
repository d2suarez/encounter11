We have reflected and discussed religious persecution throughout the history of the Bible, but religious persecution still happens today.

  • What are the reasons for religious persecution in our world?

People want to take away another’s freedom to choose whom they worship. Notice that this controlling mindset is the total opposite of God, who, in His great love, gives freedom and waits patiently for us to respond.

Ellen White writes about persecution of those who follow God, specifically in relation to them sharing truth:

“Those who live during the last days of this earth's history will know what it means to be persecuted for the truth's sake.”

—Maranatha (Review and Herald, 2014), 195

“The time is right upon us when persecution will come to those who proclaim the truth.”

—Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church, vol. 9 (Mountain View, Calif.: Paci c Press, 1948), p. 227
