There have been many archaeological finds concerning the Word of God, or the Scriptures, over the years. Search the Internet for the phrase “Word of God” and answer the following questions:

  1. Of the entries that deal with the written or spoken Word of God, what can you conclude about the concept “Word of God"?
  2. What is the Bible’s response to those who take notice of the Word of God and those who don’t? (See Proverbs 30:5; Isaiah 55:11; John 6:63; Psalm 119:50; Psalm 119:103)
  3. What sort of response do you think God and His Bible writers would like us to have about reading and responding to the Word of God?
